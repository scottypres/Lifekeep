# ⭐ THE IMPERATIVES
### Lifekeep's 5 Ranked Priorities That Determine Success

---

## #1 — PRICE COMPARISON & DEAL FINDING (Existential)

This is the single most important feature. If someone scans their HVAC unit, sees they need a 20x20x1 MERV 11 filter, and the app shows them one Amazon link at $22 — but they know they can get a 6-pack at Home Depot for $18 per filter — they'll never buy through the app again. Worse, they'll stop trusting the recommendations entirely.

This feature needs to show real-time pricing across Amazon, Home Depot, Lowe's, Walmart, AutoZone, and specialty retailers. Show the best per-unit price, flag bulk deals, highlight when something is on sale, and let users choose where to buy. If you always find them the best deal, they'll always buy through you — not because you locked them in, but because you earned it.

**The rule: People can smell the difference between a helpful tool and a sales funnel.**

---

## #2 — SCAN ACCURACY & SPECIFICITY (First Impression)

When someone scans a product and the app returns the wrong filter size or generic search results, trust is broken immediately and permanently. The AI identification has to be right, the part specs have to be exact, and the product recommendations have to actually fit.

Every wrong recommendation should be treated as a bug, not an acceptable error rate. The training system exists to systematically eliminate these errors.

---

## #3 — ONE-TAP SCAN-TO-PURCHASE FLOW

The gap between "you need a 20x20x1 MERV 11 filter" and actually having one in your hands should be as small as possible. Scan → see the best price across retailers → tap → buy → delivery tracked → reminder updated.

Every extra step between "I know what I need" and "I bought it" is a leak in the funnel where people leave and buy it on their own.

---

## #4 — SMART, NON-ANNOYING REMINDERS

If the app nags people with individual push notifications for every item, it gets silenced within a month. Reminders need to be batched, calm, and genuinely helpful.

The tone: "Here's what's coming up, here are the parts if you need them" — NOT "OVERDUE: CHANGE YOUR HVAC FILTER."

This is the retention feature. It's what keeps people opening the app month after month.

---

## #5 — HOUSEHOLD SHARING

Both partners see the same dashboard, both can log completions, both can see what the other has done. This is the emotional hook and the organic growth engine.

The bar here is "works reliably" not "revolutionary."

---

## THE CORE PHILOSOPHY

**If the app finds people better deals than they'd find on their own, AND the scan is accurate enough that they trust the recommendations, everything else follows.**

- Affiliate revenue flows because people genuinely want to buy through you
- Retention stays high because every reminder is an opportunity to save money
- Word of mouth happens because "this app saved me $40 on filters" is a story people tell

**If the deals are mediocre and the scan is sometimes wrong, no amount of beautiful UI or clever features saves it.**

---

## WHAT DOESN'T NEED TO BE BEST-IN-CLASS

- Onboarding flow — needs to be good, not perfect
- UI design — needs to be clean, not award-winning
- Training/dev tools — internal, not user-facing
- Custom reminder feature — accommodation, not selling point

---

## SCOPE DECISIONS

**Core (actively develop, market, optimize):**
- Physical product scanning and identification
- Maintenance schedule generation
- Multi-retailer price comparison with affiliate links
- Product-specific reminders
- Warranty tracking
- Receipt/purchase storage
- Household sharing

**Accommodation (exists so users don't hit a wall, minimal investment):**
- Custom reminders for health, financial, personal items
- Manual item entry without scanning

**Excluded from v1:**
- Email scanning (privacy barrier, Google audit cost)
- Health tracking platform
- Subscription management (Rocket Money's territory)
- Financial document management

**Revenue model:**
- Core features drive affiliate commerce (the business)
- Subscription unlocks premium features (supplements affiliate revenue)
- Accommodation features generate no revenue but prevent churn
